Our History

Founded in 1998, Shkolla Iliria has grown from a small local school to one of Albania's leading educational institutions. For over two decades, we have been committed to providing exceptional education that combines traditional values with modern teaching methods.

Throughout our journey, we have continuously evolved our curriculum and facilities to meet the changing needs of our students while maintaining our core commitment to academic excellence and character development.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution that nurtures future leaders who are intellectually curious, morally upright, and globally minded.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive education that develops students' academic abilities, character, and leadership skills in a supportive and innovative learning environment.
