Academic Programs

From early childhood through high school, we offer a continuum of excellence

Preschool Program (Ages 3-5)

Our play-based learning environment nurtures curiosity and builds foundational skills through engaging, age-appropriate activities.

  • Literacy and numeracy readiness
  • Social-emotional development
  • Creative expression through arts
  • Physical coordination activities

Elementary School (Grades 1-5)

We build strong academic foundations while fostering a love of learning through hands-on experiences and project-based learning.

  • Core subjects: Math, Science, Language Arts, Social Studies
  • Foreign language introduction (English, French)
  • STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Math) integration
  • Character education and leadership development

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Our transitional program helps students develop critical thinking skills and academic independence as they prepare for high school.

  • Advanced coursework in core subjects
  • Electives in technology, arts, and languages
  • Advisory program for social-emotional support
  • Extracurricular and leadership opportunities

High School (Grades 9-12)

Our college-preparatory program offers rigorous academics, specialized tracks, and personalized college counseling.

  • Advanced Placement (AP) and honors courses
  • STEM, Humanities, and Business concentrations
  • University preparation and counseling
  • Internship and research opportunities
