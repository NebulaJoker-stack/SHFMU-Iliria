Our Story, Values, and Commitment

Discover the foundation of excellence that makes Shkolla Iliria a leader in education and character development.

Our Mission & Vision

At Shkolla Iliria, we are dedicated to cultivating intellectual curiosity, moral integrity, and leadership in every student. Our mission is to provide an education that transcends traditional learning, preparing students to meet the challenges of tomorrow with confidence and creativity.

Our vision is to be recognized as a beacon of educational excellence, where innovation meets tradition, and where every student discovers their unique potential to make a meaningful impact in the world.

Our History

From humble beginnings to becoming a center of educational excellence

1998 - Founding

Shkolla Iliria was established with just 50 students and 5 teachers, with a vision to transform education in Albania.

2005 - First Expansion

We expanded our campus to accommodate growing demand, adding science labs and a library.

2012 - International Recognition

Our students began winning international competitions, putting Shkolla Iliria on the global education map.

2020 - Digital Transformation

We implemented cutting-edge technology across our curriculum, preparing students for the digital age.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Shkolla Iliria

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in academics, character development, and all school activities.

Community

We foster a supportive environment where students, teachers, and families work together.

Integrity

We value honesty, ethical behavior, and taking responsibility for our actions.

Global Perspective

We prepare students to be citizens of the world with cultural awareness and understanding.
